Garlic butter Pasta

Description

Garlic butter Pasta is a simple yet delicious dish.

Follow this recipe for a quick filling of your stomach.

Ingredients

  • 200 g pasta
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • Salt & pepper
  • parmesan/parsley/chili flakes
  1. Boil pasta in salted water, drain (keep a bit of pasta water).
  2. Melt butter in a pan, add garlic â€” cook 30 sec.
  3. Add pasta and a splash of pasta water.
  4. Mix well, season with salt and pepper.
  5. Top with parmesan, parsley or chilli flakes .
