Scrambled Eggs Recipe

Description

Scrambled eggs are a classic quick easy and tasty.

Follow this recipe to have your breakfast ready in minutes.

Ingredients

  • 3 Eggs
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • Salt & Blackpepper
  1. Crack the eggs into a Bowl
  2. Add salt and pepper
  3. Whisk untill its smooth and flightly frothy
  4. Heat a pan over medium-low heat and melt the butter
  5. Pour in the Egg mixture
  6. Stir gently with a Spatula and scrape from the edges into the middle when they start to set
  7. Cook slowly untill it becomes creamy and soft (it will keep firming after you take them off the stove)
  8. Serve
