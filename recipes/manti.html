Manti Recipe

Description

Manti is a food that originates from turkey its Dumplings filled with ground beef topped

and with garlicky yogurt which you finish by adding a special tomato sauce on top.

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups of all-purpose Flour
  • 1 egg
  • 0,5 teaspoon salt
  • 0,5 cup of water
  • 1-2 cups of plain yogurt
  • 1-2 cloves garlic
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 teaspoon pul biber
  • dried mint
  • Sumac
  • extra chilli flakes
  1. In a bowl,mix flour and salt
  2. Add the egg and slowly pour water while kneading
  3. Knead until the dough is smooth and firm
  4. Cover and rest it for 30 minutes.
  5. Mix the minced meat, grated onion, salt, and pepper in a small bowl.
  6. Divide dough in half and roll one piece very thin.
  7. Cut into small squares (~2–3 cm each).
  8. Place a tiny bit of filling in the center of each square.
  9. Pinch the opposite corners together tightly to seal.
  10. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  11. Add the mantı and boil for 7–8 minutes until soft but not falling apart.
  12. Drain gently.
  13. Mix yogurt, garlic, and salt in another seperate bowl.
  14. Melt olive oil in a pan, add pul biber, and stir for a few seconds.
  15. Place manti on plate/s.
  16. Pour garlicky yoghurt sauce over them.
  17. Drizzle with the hot olive oil.
  18. Sprinkle dried mint, sumac or chilli flakes as you wish.
